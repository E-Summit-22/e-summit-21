ABOUT US


   E-Summit is a three day event, started as an initiative of the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Bhubaneswar. The Summit seeks to facilitate interaction amonag industrialists, funding agencies, seasoned and upcoming entrepreneurs, academia and students of the participating institutes.

   E-Summit’21 will include a gamut of activities such as entrepreneurial guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions with experts, startup internship fair and investors drive. E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between Entrepreneurship and management. It is the stage for future Industry leaders and Entrepreneurs to exercise their skills.

"Where Investors Meets Innovators"

THEME


Reign of Resilience


   It has been observed that recessions and epidemics wipe out many small scale industries and startups. The outbreak of the COVID - 19 pandemic is another such depression that disrupted the global economy and affected entrepre neurs in unprecedented ways. However, resilience has emerged as a very important entrepreneurial skill in the wake of this unfortunate crisis. Resilience is required for a company to adapt and grow stronger in the face of challenges, and that is best shown by the new and creative methods adopted by various entrepreneurs worldwide to tackle this pandemic

    We at E Summit believe that this trait of an entrepreneur should be celebrated. Our intention in having this theme is to honor the startups which showed a great amount of resilience during these tough times as well as promote this quality among young students of the nation.
